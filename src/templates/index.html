Canteen

ISEC has a canteen where the social dish is served, where the prices charged are 2.65€ for students and 4.10€ for employees. In order to ensure students the possibility of having qualitatively balanced and affordable meals, SASIPC currently has 5 canteens (one operated by the services themselves and four concessionaires) presenting a total capacity of 872 seats. In addition to the canteens, all Schools/Institutes have a bar within their facilities, whose opening hours usually follow the period of school activities. For more information access here.
